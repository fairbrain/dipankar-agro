About Us

STARTED WITH TELECOM SECTOR ON 2013 AS NAMED TRISHNA TELECOM.

THEN 2015 INCORPORATED TRISHNA ENT. FOR EMAMI, ADANI, SALONI OIL, SAFED DETERGENT AS DISTRIBUTION.

THEN 2022 STARTED WITH MANUFACTURING OF CATTLE FEED OR KHESARI BESAN TO PENETRATE IN THE LOCAL MARKET TERRITORY.

THEN 2023 INCORPORATED THE ORGANIZATION NAMED “TRISHNA AGRO FOOD PVT. LTD” WITH AGRO COMMODITIES AND SNACKS PRODUCTS FOR WIDE RANGE.

About Us – Trishna Brand

Trishna began its journey in 2013 with the launch of Trishna Telecom in the telecom sector. Expanding its business horizon, Trishna Enterprises was incorporated in 2015 to serve as a distributor for renowned brands such as Emami, Adani, Saloni Oil, and Safed Detergent.

In 2022, the company ventured into manufacturing, introducing cattle feed and Khesari Besan to establish a strong presence in the local market. Building on this growth, Dipankar Agro Food Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in 2023, marking Trishna's expansion into agro commodities and snack products, offering a diverse range to cater to a wider consumer base. With a legacy of quality and trust, Trishna continues to evolve, bringing excellence in every sector it steps into.
