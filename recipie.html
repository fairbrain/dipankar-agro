Suji Upma (Semolina Upma) Ingredients :
  • 1 cup suji (semolina)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
Instructions :
  1. Roast the Suji:
    • In a dry pan, lightly roast the suji on low to medium heat, stirring constantly.
    • Roast until it becomes light golden brown, then set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Tempering:
    • Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
    • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
    • Add dried red chilies and sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add the Vegetables:
    • Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
    • Add the chopped mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
    • Add turmeric powder and salt to taste.
  4. Cook the Suji:
    • Add water to the pan and bring it to a boil.
    • Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to medium-low.
    • Gradually add the roasted suji, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
    • Keep stirring and cook for 3-4 minutes until the suji absorbs the water and becomes soft.
  5. Cook and Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and add a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
    • Serve hot as a tasty breakfast option.

Enjoy your delicious suji breakfast! You can customize it by adding your favorite veggies or spices for extra flavor.
